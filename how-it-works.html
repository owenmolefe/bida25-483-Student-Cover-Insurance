How It Works

Getting covered is simple. Follow these 3 steps.

1

Choose Your Plan

Browse our cover plans and select the one that fits your devices and budget. Basic, Standard, or Premium.

2

Request a Quote

Fill out our simple quote form. Tell us which devices you want to insure and your preferred plan.

3

Get Covered

We'll contact you within 24 hours with your personalized quote. Once confirmed, you're protected.
