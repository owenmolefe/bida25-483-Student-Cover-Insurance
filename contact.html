About Student Cover Insurance

Student Cover Insurance was founded by students, for students. We understand the challenges of university life and how expensive it can be to replace a stolen or damaged laptop, phone, or tablet.

Our mission is to provide affordable, simple, and reliable gadget insurance that fits a student's budget. No hidden fees. No complicated forms. Just protection when you need it most.

We are based in Gaborone, Botswana and serve students across all major universities and colleges.

Why Choose Us?

  • ✅ Affordable plans from P50/month
  • ✅ Fast claims processing within 48 hours
  • ✅ Cover for theft, accidental damage, and loss
  • ✅ Student-friendly customer support
